Lost Objects

Seeku Is a service for EAFIT users who have lost their objects in campus. It is called Seeku and it is a web page that will allow users to find their objects and reclaim them from a device connected to the internet. Unlike looking for the object in person at the lost objects place, our service saves time and effort for users to find their lost objects since they can use the web page to find out if their object was found.
